Miso Soup

1. Place water in a medium sauce pan over low heat.
2. Add nori and cook until the mixture to simmer (around 5-7 minutes).
3. In the meantime, place 3 Tbsp of miso into a small bowl, add a little hot water and whisk until smooth. Then add to the soup and stir.
4. Add remaining ingredients to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes or so.
5. Taste and add more miso or a pinch of sea salt if desired. Serve warm.

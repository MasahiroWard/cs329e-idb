Indian Cuisine

the India culture has a variety of different religious in different place,so Indian Cuisine is also affected by its diverse traditions. Hinduism and Islam are two major religions that affect the development of India's diet culture.In recent history, English, Portuguese and Persian also have a huge influence on indian cuisine.
In India, being a vegetarian is a very common thing. Mughlai, which is the Hui food, there are many Muslim immigrants are brought into India, including kababs (meat roasted or stewed),aroma of curry and meatballs and rice - a mixture of rice and meat food, of course, mud furnace Tanzania Duli (Tandoori Roast Chicken).In addition, India also has a lot of food from europe.
By the range of diversity in climate, soil type, the Indian cuisines have some quite different form each other.Therefore, different location in India would use locally materials, vegetables, fruits and spices so there is no so-called orthodox cuisine.
Although different families has different eating habits, rice is the staple food for every family to eat. For example, the dish made of gravy is also very popular in India, which known as curry, even it has different tastes in each region.
